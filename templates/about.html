What is LifeTag?

LifeTag is an innovative, secure, and user-friendly platform designed to revolutionize how we access and manage emergency medical information. In critical situations, every second counts. LifeTag ensures that vital health data is immediately available to first responders and medical professionals, bridging the information gap and potentially saving lives.

Our system goes beyond simple data storage; it creates a dynamic link between individuals and their crucial medical history, accessible through a quick QR code scan. This allows for informed decisions, faster treatment, and better patient outcomes during emergencies.

Core Features and How It Works

Personalized User Accounts

Users can easily create Aadhaar based secure accounts to manage their profiles, ensuring privacy and control over their data.

Comprehensive Emergency Medical Info

Add critical details like allergies, blood type, existing medical conditions and current medications.

Emergency Contact Details

Store info of contacts that you want to get notified when in any emergency. Allows anyone to contact them in case of emergency.

Diagnostic Role-Based Access

Verified medical professionals gain secure, role-based access to update user medical records, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.

Secure & Role-Based QR Scanners

LifeTag employs a dual-scanner system to maintain stringent security and ensure appropriate access levels. This means different sets of information are revealed based on who is scanning, protecting your sensitive data.

General Public Scanner

Designed for the **general public** or first responders, this scanner provides instant access to essential, non-sensitive emergency information. This includes basic details like emergency contacts, known allergies, and critical medical conditions, without revealing private diagnostic records.

Open Public Scanner

Verified Medical Rep Scanner

Exclusively for **authorized medical representatives** and healthcare providers. This scanner requires secure authentication and provides access to detailed medical records, including diagnostic reports, full medical history, and treatment plans, enabling comprehensive care.

Open Verified Scanner

Why LifeTag Matters

In the chaos of an emergency, seconds can define outcomes. Traditional methods of accessing medical history are often slow or nonexistent, leading to critical delays in diagnosis and treatment. LifeTag addresses this by providing an immediate, reliable bridge between patients and medical responders.

Imagine a scenario where an unconscious patient is found. With LifeTag, paramedics can quickly scan a QR code on a wearable or a card, instantly gaining access to crucial information like blood type, allergies, or existing conditions. This immediate insight enables them to administer the correct first aid and transport the patient to the most appropriate facility without guesswork. For hospitals, verified access means a complete medical history is available, streamlining admissions and treatment plans. LifeTag isn't just a convenience; it's a critical tool for enhanced patient safety and efficient healthcare delivery.
